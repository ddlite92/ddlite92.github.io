ABOUT ME

I'm an LRC Artist turned developer, passionate about building tools that enhance render and lighting pipelines through code. My work lives at the intersection of lighting artistry and technical development, creating solutions that to help teams while pushing technical boundaries.

With 8+ years in animation industry, I've developed a unique hybrid skillset that combines artistic vision with technical problem-solving. My experience spans the full animation pipeline - from animating on Didi & Friends into optimizing lighting workflows for animated series and films - giving me rare insight to where technical solutions can most impact creative work.

I'm currently focused on Python development and pipeline tools, having earned certifications from MDEC and Google. My goal is to transition into a technical artist or tools developer role where I can apply both my production experience and growing programming skills to build better creative pipelines.

When I'm not debugging shaders or solving CodeWars problems, you'll find me experimenting with Blender's Python API, contributing to open-source animation tools, or prototyping lighting automation scripts - always looking for ways to bridge the gap between art and code.
